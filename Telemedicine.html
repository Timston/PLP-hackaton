About Telemedicine

Telemedicine is the use of technology to deliver healthcare services remotely. This can include consultations with healthcare providers via video, remote monitoring of patients, and digital health records. It helps increase access to healthcare, especially for rural and underserved populations.

Telemedicine Articles

Telemedicine in Action

Telemedicine allows doctors to diagnose and treat patients remotely. With the use of video calls, remote monitoring, and digital tools, patients can receive care in the comfort of their homes.

The Future of Telemedicine

Telemedicine is rapidly evolving with advancements in AI, remote diagnostics, and wearable health devices. In the future, it is expected to become a staple of modern healthcare.
